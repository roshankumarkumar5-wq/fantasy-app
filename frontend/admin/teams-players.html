Matches Teams & Players OTP Lookup

Add a Real Team

Needs to be a public image link. No logo image ready? Try https://placehold.co/128x128?text=MI (swap "MI" for your team's code) as a free working placeholder, or upload your own image to a free host like imgur.com and paste its direct image link here.

Bulk Upload Players (CSV)

CSV columns required: name, real_team_id, role, photo_url (photo_url optional, e.g. https://i.pravatar.cc/150?img=1 as a free placeholder). Role must be one of: batsman, bowler, all-rounder, keeper.

Add a Single Player

Needs to be a public image link. Try https://i.pravatar.cc/150?img=1 (change the number for a different face) as a free working placeholder while testing.
