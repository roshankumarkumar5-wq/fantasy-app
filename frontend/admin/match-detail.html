1. Lock Selections

Selection closes automatically 1 hour before the match (no manual action needed for that). Use this button if you want to lock it early for any reason.

2. Upload Scoresheet PDF (generates a CSV for you)

Upload the official match scoresheet PDF. It's stored for your records, and best-effort parsed into a CSV of runs/wickets/catches/stumpings/run-outs per player (works well with CricHeroes-style scorecards). Player names are the key — download the CSV, fix anything that looks wrong in Excel, then upload it in step 3.

3. Upload Final Stats CSV

Upload the CSV — either the one generated in step 2 (edited/corrected), or one you build yourself. Columns: player_name, player_id, runs, wickets, catches, stumpings, run_outs (player_id is optional — matching is done by name). This is what unlocks the Finalize step below.

Or enter stats manually

Alternative to the CSV upload above — add a row per player and pick them by name.

4. Finalize Match & Compute Points

Calculates every user's team total and marks the match completed. Blocked until final stats have been saved or uploaded (step 3 or the manual entry above).
